About Me

I am a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University. I obtained my B.Sc in Electrical Engineering (Summa Cum Laude) from The Ohio State University.

My research interests are in Machine Learning and Signal Processing. I use ideas from theory and traditional engineering methods to design and build better learning models. Recently I’ve started tinckering with Graph Neural Networks.
